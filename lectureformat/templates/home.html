Welcome to elecCalc.

This calculator is an early prototype of our group project elecTUM of

TUM:Junge Akademie. Learn more about our project through our website.

DISCLAIMER: This website is only a prototype and still undergoing construction.

You will most likely encounter thinks which seem to not work properly and links that will lead to error messages.

We are aware of most functionallity breaking bugs, but if you think you e.g. found an interesting edge case during the calculation which we didn't think ouf, please let us know. The best way you can do so is by opening an issue over on our GitHub. This is also where you can help us by contributing to our source code!

Find out the most sustainable option for your lectures.

Calculate their energy consumption and take climate action.

With our calculator, you can determine the most energy efficient option for your lectures. If you have any questions, do not hesitate to contact us at electum@ja.tum.de.

Step 4
Select your sampling mode and additional outputs.
Here you can choose between simple or advanced mcmc sampling methods. There is also the option to include further additional parameters such as living situation, type of devices used etc.

Decide which option you want to test
In this final step, you can choose which test option you want to calculate. Here you can select among offline, online streaming, online video-on-demand (VoD), hybrid streaming and hybrid video-on-demand options.
