Who are we?

As part of the scholarship program “TUM: Junge Akademie” of the Technical University of Munich, our project started in 2020, amidst the Covid-19 pandemic. Unsatisfied with online teaching formats at the time and curious about how their carbon footprint compared to onsite formats, we decided to investigate the energy consumption of lectures at TUM.

We are specifically interested in finding out what hybrid format between online and on-site lectures maximizes the energy efficiency of university teaching, and how our institution can reduce its impact on the planet in this disruptive situation.
